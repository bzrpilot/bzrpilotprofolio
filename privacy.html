Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 12, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit my portfolio website. I value your privacy and aim to be transparent about the minimal data collected.

Information Collected

Contact Form: When you submit a message via the contact form, I collect the name, email address, and message content you provide. This information is processed through Google Forms for the purpose of responding to your inquiry.

AI Assistants: When you interact with the AI assistants (Cinephile.ai, etc.), your messages are processed through our backend to provide responses. These interactions are session-based and designed to be transient.

Use of Information

The information collected is used solely to respond to your inquiries and improve the AI interaction experience. I do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

I use Google Forms for form submissions and Groq for AI processing. These services have their own privacy policies regarding data handling.

Security

I implement security-first practices to protect your data, including the use of HTTPS for all communications and secure handling of API keys on the server side.

Contact Me

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact me through the contact form on the main page.
