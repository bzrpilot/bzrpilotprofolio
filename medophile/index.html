The Medophile

Music is my only addiction — and I’m okay with that.

Music as a Constant

I listen to music almost 24/7. It’s not just background noise; it’s an essential part of my environment. While I don’t smoke or drink, music is my only real addiction, and it’s one I embrace fully.

Whether I’m diving deep into a security audit or learning a new skill, music fuels my focus. It sharpens my concentration, regulates my emotions, and keeps me grounded in a world that often feels chaotic.

Music in Every State

Music is my constant companion through every phase of life. It’s there while I’m traveling through new landscapes, and it’s there when I feel low or overwhelmed by the noise of the world. Even when I’m trying to sleep or simply want to be alone, music provides the perfect sanctuary.

In moments where silence feels too loud, music fills the void with clarity and purpose.

Taste & Evolution

My music taste is random and constantly evolving. I don’t believe in limiting myself to specific genres — I limit myself only to feeling. If a track resonates, it finds a place in my rotation.

That said, I mostly gravitate toward hip-hop, rap, and beat-driven music. There’s something about the rhythm and the flow that aligns perfectly with my analytical mindset.

Early Memory

The first foreign / English song I remember was “Baby” by Justin Bieber. I don’t remember the exact year, but it was around the time the song was released and taking over the world. That moment was a catalyst, pushing me to look beyond local sounds and explore global music.

Discovery Phase

Later, I moved into a deep discovery phase, exploring artists and bands like One Direction, Ed Sheeran, and other popular acts that defined the 2010–2015 era. During this time, music transitioned from being mere entertainment into a lifelong habit.

Playlist Era

Today, my music taste lives through my Spotify playlists. My listening patterns, moods, and phases are all reflected there. Each playlist is a reflection of a specific time or feeling, fueling my creativity and focus in everything I do.

Spotify Wrapped & Influence

Every year, Spotify Wrapped feels like opening a time capsule. It’s a reflection of the moods, moments, and music that shaped my year. Music isn’t just sound to me; it shapes my world, my creativity, my discipline, and my inner world.
